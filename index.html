StarCraft II ladder maps

Welcome! This website is for StarCraft II players who FINISHED campaign or have a history with RTS genre
and are looking for basic informations, tips and tricks about current 1v1 ladder map pool

If you feel feel like this fits you, go ahead and take a look at the maps!
Cosmic Sapphire LE,  Data-C LE,  Inside and Out LE,  Moondance LE,  Stargazers LE,  Tropical Sacrifice LE,  Waterfall LE

The "LE" after each map name stands for "Ladder Edition"
This means that the map came through TL.net Map Contest (current map pool came from #16) and was afterwards approved by Blizzard themselves
In few cases, the map was slightly changed by Blizzard before implementation to better fit the competetive level of play
